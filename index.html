Research and Development Manpower Full Time Equivalence by Sector in Singapore

Visualize and analyse sector wise, year wise, manpower wise full time equivalance data in Research and Development in Singapore. One full-time equivalence (FTE) unit may be thought of as one person-year. A person who spends 30% of his time on R&D and the rest on other activities during the one-year reporting period should be considered as 0.3 FTE. If a fulltime R&D worker is employed for only six months during the one year reporting period, this results in a 0.5 FTE
